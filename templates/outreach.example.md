## Recruiter / hiring-manager follow-ups
For roles with a named job poster, send a short follow-up ~3–5 days after applying if no response:
**Template:**
> Hi <Name>, I applied for the <Role> position a few days ago and wanted to express my genuine interest.
> I'm a <role> (<stack>) with <experience>. I'd welcome the chance to discuss how I can contribute —
> happy to share more or do a quick call. Thank you!
