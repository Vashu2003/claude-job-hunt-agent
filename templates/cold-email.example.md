# Cold Email — HR / Founders Outreach

> Proactive emails to HR / hiring managers / founders, for (a) companies you've already applied to (reinforce
> interest) and (b) net-new companies matching your criteria (create opportunities that aren't even posted).
> The agent DRAFTS + personalizes each one and saves it as a Gmail draft; YOU review & send (sending is kept
> manual to protect email deliverability and stay non-spammy).

## Criteria (edit to your situation)
- **Order:** (1) FIRST cold-email companies you've ALREADY APPLIED to (reinforce interest). (2) THEN net-new.
- **Net-new target filter (any of):**
  - Companies (remote OR onsite) that **received funding recently**.
  - Companies within **commuting distance of your home** — onsite-convenient.
  - Strong stack-fit product companies / junior-friendly startups (agent's judgment).
- **Email angle:** warm intro + 1-line concrete impact + ask for a quick chat or to be considered. Be honest about your experience level.
- **Send-from:** <your-application-email@example.com>.
- **Mechanism:** create Gmail DRAFTS to review & send (protects deliverability; mass cold-send from a personal
  Gmail risks spam-flagging). Flip to auto-send only if you explicitly choose to.
- **Daily volume:** keep low (~3–5/day) to protect sender reputation.
- **Contacts:** NEVER send to a guessed address; only verified/listed HR/founder emails, flagged as such.

## How to find contacts (no paid tools)
- From the JD / company "About"/Careers page / LinkedIn "job poster" name.
- Common patterns: firstname@company.com, firstname.lastname@company.com, hr@/careers@/jobs@/hello@company.com
  — flag these as "(guessed)"; never send to a guessed address.

## Draft template (edit to your background)
> Subject: <Your Role> (<key skills>) — interested in <Company>
>
> Hi <Name>,
>
> I'm <Your Name>, a <role> in <city>. Over the past <duration> I shipped <1–2 concrete, quantified wins —
> e.g. "a KYC onboarding flow (FastAPI + React)" and "an AI workflow that cut response time 60%">.
> Stack: <your core stack>.
>
> I admire what <Company> is building <1 specific line about the company> and would love to contribute as a
> <role>. Would you be open to a quick chat, or could you point me to whoever owns engineering hiring?
> Resume attached.
>
> Thanks for your time,
> <Your Name> · <email> · <phone> · <linkedin>
